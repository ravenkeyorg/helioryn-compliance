# Helioryn Intelligence Topic Strategy

## Selection Criteria

Choose intelligence domains that:
- Change rapidly
- Have conflicting sources
- Require continuous monitoring
- Influence money, risk, regulation, or strategy
- Are difficult to monitor manually
