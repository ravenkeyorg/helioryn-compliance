Here's a progression of demo questions, easy to hard:
Easy (single doc, fact lookup)
- "What were the questioned costs in the Crisis Intervention Iowa audit?"
- "How many recommendations were made for Virginia DCJS?"
- "What PII issues were found in the UM PGHC Maryland audit?"
Medium (synthesis across 2-3 docs)
- "What are the most common types of findings in VOCA grant audits?"
- "Which audits found problems with subrecipient monitoring?"
- "Compare the questioned costs between Safe Horizon and Crisis Intervention Iowa"
Hard (cross-reference, nuance)
- "Is there a pattern of inadequate written policies across the OIG audits?"
- "What recommendations involve personnel cost documentation?"
- "Which grantees had issues with federal fund accounting, and what was recommended?"
Very Hard (verifier stress test)
- "What compliance gaps appear in multiple audits that could indicate systemic issues?"
- "If I'm a VOCA grantee, what should I fix first based on these findings?"
Tips
- The gov search toggle isn't useful right now (FAC rate limited)
- The verifier is strict — expect "plausible" rather than "verified" for most answers because the LLM rephrases things rather than quoting verbatim
- Broad questions like "Tell me about VOCA" will get confused by the news article noise despite the boost — keep questions specific to the OIG data
